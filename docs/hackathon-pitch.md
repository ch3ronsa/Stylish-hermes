# Hackathon Pitch

## One-Liner

Stylish Hermes is an AI stylist that turns inspiration images into wearable, real-life outfit guidance.

## 30-Second Version

People constantly save outfit inspiration from Pinterest, anime, editorials, and social media, but they still do not know how to translate that into something wearable. Stylish Hermes solves that. You give it a reference image, and it tells you the aesthetic, the palette, the occasion fit, and a simpler real-life version you can actually wear. It is built as a Hermes skill, so it can later expand into shopping advice, weather-aware styling, and optional wardrobe tracking.

## Problem

- inspiration is easy to collect
- interpretation is hard
- most users want actionable styling, not just fashion labels

## Solution

- image in
- style analysis out
- wearable outfit translation out

## Why It Matters

It closes the gap between taste and execution.
